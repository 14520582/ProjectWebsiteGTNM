Ferdinand (John Cena) is a bull who would rather smell flowers than fight toreadors in the ring. When people see him, they immediately perceive him as a big, scary beast, which is why he's taken from his quaint village to become a fighting bull. However, he just doesn't have the inclination to be rough and tough.
